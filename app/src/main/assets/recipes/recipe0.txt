title:Pancakes
step:Mix 2 ½ flour with ½ tbsp salt in a bowl.
step:Whip the flour mix together with half of the milk (6 dl). Then whip together the mix with the rest of the milk and the 3 eggs.
step:Melt part of the butter in the pan and pour out a thin layer of mix to cover the whole pan. Fry for 30 seconds on each side until finishing all of the mix. (10 min)
timer:00.12.07
step:Serve with jam, berries or fruit.
